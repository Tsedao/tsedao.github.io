I am an incoming Ph.D. student at Department of Computer Science, Purdue University, and I am fortunated be awarded by Lynn Fellowship. I am currently a Research Associate at the Agent Mediated Intelligence (AMI) Research Group under the supervsion of Prof. Bo An at Nanyang Technological University (NTU). I obtained my master degree at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ) in Data Science under the supervison of Prof. Shuang Li. I obtained my bachelor degree at Xi'an Jiao Tong Liverpool University (XJTLU) in Applied Mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Jia Meng and Prof. Jionglong Su.

Research

I have broad interests in the Sequential Decision Making, Machine Learning, and LLMs. Especially, I focus on the following directions:

  • Large-scale optimization for scientific discovery, i.e., biological sequence optimization and material discovery.
  • RL as Probabilistic Inference, scale and accelerate probabilistic sampling algorithms, i.e., SMC, MCMC, by leveraging world/prior knowledge in LLM/VLMs and diffusion models.
