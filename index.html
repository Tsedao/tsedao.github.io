Short Bio

I am an incoming Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, Purdue University and I am fortunate to be awarded the Lynn Fellowship. I am currently a Research Associate at the Agent Mediated Intelligence (AMI) Research Group under the supervision of Prof. Bo An at Nanyang Technological University (NTU). I obtained my master's degree in Data Science at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-SZ) under the supervision of Prof. Shuang Li. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU) under the supervision of Prof. Jia Meng and Prof. Jionglong Su.

Research

I have broad interests in Sequential Decision Making, Machine Learning, and LLMs. Especially, I focus on the following directions:

  • Large-scale optimization for scientific discovery, i.e., biological sequence optimization, material discovery and perference learning.
  • RL as Probabilistic Inference, scale and accelerate probabilistic sampling algorithms, i.e., SMC, MCMC, by leveraging world/prior knowledge in LLM/VLMs and diffusion models.
